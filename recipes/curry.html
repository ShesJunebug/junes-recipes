Curry

This flavor-packed curry is budget-friendly, great for leftovers, and low on dishes.
It can also be used as a base for many other sauces!

Ingredients to Gather:

Spice Mix:

1⁄2 tsp Cayenne
1 tsp Turmeric
1 tsp Cumin
1 Tbsp Coriander

Additional Ingredients:

1 Large Onion
10oz Firm Tofu
1in Ginger
5 Cloves of Garlic
14oz Can of Crushed Tomatoes
14oz Can of Coconut Milk
14oz Can of Chickpeas drained
1 tsp Garam Masala
1 tsp Sugar
1 tsp Salt
2 C Dry Rice
Oil of your choice
Fresh Cilantro optional
Juice of 1⁄2 Lemon optional
Naan Bread optional

Let. Them. Cook!

  1. Add equal parts 2 C dry rice and water to an instant pot. Set the instant pot to high pressure for 3 minutes.
  2. Remove the rice and set aside.
    • At this point, you may scoop out the rice you want to serve and refrigerate the remaining rice.
  3. Add 2 Tbsp of oil to the instant pot and set it to Saute mode.
  4. Peel and chop the onion.
  5. Drain and cut the tofu into bite sized cubes.
  6. Peel and grate the ginger. Crush the garlic.
  7. Add both the onion and tofu into the instant pot and saute on high for 6 minutes, stirring often.
  8. Combine the ingredients for the Spice Mix together in a small bowl.
  9. When the 6-minute timer is done, add the ginger, garlic, and spice mix to the pot.
  10. Saute for 2 more minutes, stirring frequently to prevent sticking.
  11. When the 2-minute timer is done, add the coconut milk, crushed tomatoes, and drained chickpeas.
  12. Stir the pot well to deglaze and prevent a food burn error.
  13. Add the lid and set the instant pot to pressure cook on high for 10 minutes.
  14. When the 10-minute timer is done, do a quick release.
  15. Add 1 tsp each of salt, sugar, and garam masala.
    • At this point, you may also add the cilantro and lemon juice.
  16. Serve over rice and optionally scoop with Naan bread.
  17. Enjoy!
