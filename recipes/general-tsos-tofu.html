General Tso's Tofu

This sweet and savory dish is packed with flavor and will leave you craving it again and again.
General Tso's Tofu is also found to be a slightly lighter, healthier version of General Tso's Chicken.

Ingredients Needed:

  • 10oz Firm Tofu
  • 1/2C + 2 Tbsp Corn Starch
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 6 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 6 Tbsp Vegetable Stock
  • 3 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 6 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • Red Pepper Flakes to taste
  • Oil of your choice
