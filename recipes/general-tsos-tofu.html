General Tso's Tofu

This sweet and savory dish is budget-friendly, easy to customize, and low on dishes.
It can also be used as a base for many other sauces!

Ingredients to Gather:

For the Tofu:

10oz Firm Tofu
1⁄2 C Corn Starch
2 tsp Garlic Powder
2 tsp Onion Powder
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

2 Tbsp Corn Starch
6 Tbsp Brown Sugar
3 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
6 Tbsp Soy Sauce
6 Tbsp Vegetable Stock
Red Pepper Flakes to taste

Additional Ingredients:

11⁄2 C Dry Rice
Oil of your choice
3 Cloves of Garlic minced
Sesame Seeds for garnish
Scallions for garnish

Let. Them. Cook!

  1. Drain and cut the tofu into bite sized cubes.
  2. Add the tofu and spices to a bowl and mix well, ensuring that the tofu is completely covered in the spice mixture.
  3. Mix the sauce ingredients together in a small bowl
  4. Add a few tablespoons of oil to a pan over medium-high heat.
  5. At this point, add equal parts 11⁄2 C dry rice and water to an instant pot. Set the instant pot to high pressure for 3 minutes.
  6. Cook the tofu over medium-high heat until all sides are golden brown.
  7. Remove the tofu and add 3 cloves of minced garlic into the pan.
    • You may need to add more oil for this step.
  8. Cook the garlic until golden brown.
  9. Add the sauce mixture to the pan and stir frequently until the sauce has thickened.
  10. At this point, you may serve each part of the dish separately and put leftovers into their own container, or add the tofu back to the pan and incorporate it with the sauce.
  11. Add the tofu and sauce over your rice. Top with sesame seeds and scallions.
  12. Enjoy!
