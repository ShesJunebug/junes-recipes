Gumbo

This hearty, flavourful dish is easy to customize and great for leftovers.
It is quite literally my favorite dish of all time.

Ingredients to Gather:

Before You Start:

3 Yellow Onions
6oz Okra
1 Bunch of Green Onions
4 Cloves of Garlic minced
5⁄8 tsp Salt
1⁄4 tsp Pepper
1⁄8 tsp Cayenne Pepper

As You're Cooking:

4 C Chicken Broth
4 C Water
2 Bay Leaves
2 to 3 C Dehydrated Soy Curls
14oz Beyond Sausage
Cajun Seasoning of your choice to taste
Red Hot to taste

Additional Ingredients:

3 C Dry Rice
Oil of your choice

Let. Them. Cook!

  1. Dice the onions and add them to a pot with 1/8 C Oil. Caramelize them over just-above medium heat, stirring occasionaly, until they are a deep chocolate color. This is the longest step and may take 1-2 hours, but it makes or breaks the dish!
  2. While the onions are caramelizing, slice up your okra and add to a pan with a few tablespoons of oil. Heat them over medium heat, stirring occasionaly, until the slime is cooked out.
  3. While both of those steps are cooking, slice the green onions and mince the 4 cloves of garlic. You should also prep your broth, water, and bay leaves.
  4. When the onions are done caramelizing, add the green onions, okra, garlic, and 1⁄8 tsp of cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cook and stir for about 3 minutes.
  6. Add the broth, water, and bay leaves to the pot. Stir well to ensure none of the ingredients are stuck to the bottom.
  7. Add a few shakes of cajun seasoning and a few tablespoons of red hot.
  8. Bring to a boil, then let simmer with the lid on for 1 hour.
  9. While the gumbo simmers, cook the sausage in a pan and hydrate the soy curls.
    • To hydrate the soy curls, put them in a bowl with warm water and let sit for 10 minutes. Then drain the water and set them aside.
  10. After the gumbo has simmered for an hour, add the sausage, soy curls, 1⁄2 tsp salt, and 1⁄4 tsp pepper. Simmer for 30 more minutes.
  11. Add equal parts 3 C dry rice and water to an instant pot. Set the instant pot to high pressure for 3 minutes.
  12. After 30 minutes, serve over rice and top with more cajun seasoning and red hot until you find your perfect balance!
  13. Enjoy!
